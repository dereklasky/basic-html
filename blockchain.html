Introduction

My portfolio primarily consists of digital currency built on blockchains. My passion for blockchain started a long time ago in a galaxy far, far away. Since then, I have become a miner of these same crypto currencies. In basic terms, mining means I agree to pay money in the form of my electricity bill in exchange for the processing power to mine digital currencies that I can either hold to sell (e.g. store of value) and/or sell these coins immediately for profit. Recently, blockchain mining has taken me to the new digital finance ecosystem where I stake my "Ethereum" coins in exchange for newly minted Compound tokens.
